Welcome

This project aims to develop a searching platform, by which tourists in road trip can receive the recommendations of interest of places based on their input information.

This platform supports 5 different kinds of input boxes, where people can choose any of them as the computing standard of the platform.

The five distinct categories are the preferred places people want to travel, tourism time, budget, soft services and characters of tourists (teenagers prefer playground or shopping mall ).
